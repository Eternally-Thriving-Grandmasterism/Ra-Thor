## The 7 Layers of a Forest Garden (The Living Architecture)

### 1. Canopy Layer (Tall Trees — 10–30m)
Overstory trees that provide shade, wind protection, and long-term timber/nut crops.

**Examples:** Chestnut, walnut, pecan, oak, pine, apple, pear (standard rootstock)

**Mercy Role:** Creates the protective roof. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) strongest here.

### 2. Understory Layer (Small Trees — 5–10m)
Smaller fruit and nut trees that thrive in partial shade.

**Examples:** Serviceberry, pawpaw, persimmon, dwarf fruit trees, hazelnut

**Mercy Role:** Fills the light gaps. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) shines.

### 3. Shrub Layer (1.5–5m)
Berry bushes, currants, and nitrogen-fixing shrubs.

**Examples:** Blueberry, raspberry, blackcurrant, sea buckthorn, goumi, autumn olive

**Mercy Role:** Creates edge habitat and early successional abundance. Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) is loud.

### 4. Herbaceous Layer (0.5–1.5m)
Perennial vegetables, herbs, and medicinal plants.

**Examples:** Rhubarb, asparagus, comfrey, mint, lemon balm, yarrow, echinacea

**Mercy Role:** Daily harvest layer. Gate 2 (Truth-Verification) and Gate 3 (Non-Deception) are clear — you see and taste the health of the system every day.

### 5. Ground Cover Layer (0–0.5m)
Low-growing plants that protect soil and suppress weeds.

**Examples:** Strawberry, clover, creeping thyme, violets, wood sorrel

**Mercy Role:** Living mulch. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) dominate — soil is never bare.

### 6. Root Layer (Underground)
Edible roots, tubers, and dynamic accumulators.

**Examples:** Jerusalem artichoke, horseradish, comfrey (deep roots bring up minerals), skirret, oca

**Mercy Role:** Nutrient mining and soil building. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) and Gate 1 (Ethical Alignment) are strong.

### 7. Vine Layer (Climbing Plants)
Vertical space users that add another dimension of production.

**Examples:** Grape, kiwi, hardy kiwi, hops, passionfruit (in suitable climates), runner beans (annual filler)

**Mercy Role:** Maximizes vertical abundance. Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) and Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) light up.
