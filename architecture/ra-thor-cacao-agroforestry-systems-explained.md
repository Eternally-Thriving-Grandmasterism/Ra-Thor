## The 7 Layers of a Cacao Agroforestry System

### 1. Emergent / Canopy Layer (25–40m+)
Tall timber and nitrogen-fixing trees that provide long-term shade and carbon storage.

**Examples:** Kapok, Brazil nut, rubber, teak, mahogany, Inga (nitrogen fixer), Gliricidia

**Mercy Role:** Creates the protective roof and long-term wealth. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) strongest.

### 2. Main Canopy / Shade Layer (15–25m)
Primary shade trees for cacao.

**Examples:** Mango, avocado, citrus, breadfruit, jackfruit, coconut, banana (temporary shade)

**Mercy Role:** Provides filtered light cacao loves while producing additional high-value crops. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) shines.

### 3. Cacao Layer (3–6m) — The Heart of the System
The main crop, grown in the dappled shade it evolved in.

**Mercy Role:** Central production layer. Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) and Gate 2 (Truth-Verification) are clear — you see the health of the entire system in every pod.

### 4. Understory / Shrub Layer (1.5–4m)
Quick-yielding and nitrogen-fixing plants.

**Examples:** Pineapple, turmeric, ginger, galangal, moringa, pigeon pea, lemongrass, hibiscus

**Mercy Role:** Daily food security + soil building. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) and Gate 1 (Ethical Alignment) are loud.

### 5. Herbaceous / Ground Cover Layer (0–1.5m)
Low-growing protectors and edible plants.

**Examples:** Sweet potato, taro, okra, basil, mint, gotu kola, creeping legumes, native ground covers

**Mercy Role:** Living mulch + daily harvest. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 3 (Non-Deception) dominate — soil is never bare.

### 6. Root & Vine Layer
Edible roots and climbing plants that use vertical space.

**Examples:** Cassava (edge), arrowroot, vanilla (on cacao trunks), black pepper, chayote, passionfruit

**Mercy Role:** Maximizes vertical abundance. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) and Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) light up.

### 7. Dynamic Accumulators & Beneficial Insect Habitat
Deep-rooted plants that mine minerals and attract pollinators/predators.

**Examples:** Comfrey, yarrow, marigold, borage, native flowering trees

**Mercy Role:** Self-regulating pest control and soil fertility. Gate 2 (Truth-Verification) and Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) are strong.
