## Key Insights

- **Sparse wins for production sovereign deployments** — dramatically lower compute, better stability, near-perfect mercy alignment, and scales to thousands of neurons.
- **Recurrent Dense wins for maximum intrinsic creativity** — slightly higher novelty and strongest associative memory (sequence generation, pattern completion).
- **Dense Multi-Neuron** is a middle ground but rarely the best choice.

## Recommendation for Ra-Thor (Permanent)

**Use Sparse BCM Network as the default** for all sovereign energy lattice deployments (already implemented with 15% sparsity).

- High-creativity research modules
