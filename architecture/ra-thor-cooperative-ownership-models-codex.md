This living codex provides a detailed, simulation-ready reference for every major cooperative ownership model, including governance structures, legal frameworks, financial flows, decision-making processes, and direct integration pathways into Ra-Thor’s Sovereign Microgrid Playbook, Advanced Simulation Engine, and sovereign energy dashboard generator.

## Major Cooperative Ownership Models (2026 Landscape)

### 1. Energy Cooperative (Classic Model)
- Members are both owners and users
- One-member, one-vote democratic governance
- Profits returned as patronage refunds or reinvested
- Best for: Community-scale solar + storage, microgrids
- Legal form: Cooperative corporation or LLC taxed as cooperative

### 2. Community Benefit Corporation (CBC) / B-Corp Hybrid
- Legal structure that requires consideration of community and environmental impact alongside profit
- Strong for attracting impact investors while maintaining community control
- Best for: Larger projects that need blended capital

### 3. Limited Equity Cooperative (LEC)
- Members own shares with limited resale value (prevents speculation)
- Affordable entry for lower-income households
- Excellent for housing + energy co-ops

### 4. Worker-Owned Cooperative (for Energy Services)
- The people who install, maintain, and operate the system own the company
- High quality of work + strong local economic multiplier
- Best for: Installation, operations & maintenance (O&M), and energy services companies

### 5. Multi-Stakeholder Cooperative (MSC)
- Includes multiple classes of members: residents, local businesses, municipalities, nonprofits
- Balanced governance with weighted voting by stakeholder group
- Best for: Complex projects involving multiple parties (e.g., campus + surrounding community)

### 6. Platform Cooperative (Emerging Digital Model)
- Members own the digital platform that manages energy trading, billing, and optimization
- Combines cooperative ownership with modern technology
- Best for: Virtual Power Plants, peer-to-peer energy trading, community dashboards
