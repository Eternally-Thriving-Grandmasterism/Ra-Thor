## The 7 Layers of a Silvopasture System

### 1. Canopy / Shade Trees (15–30m+)
Tall trees that provide shade, wind protection, and long-term timber or fruit.

**Examples:** Inga, Gliricidia, Leucaena, oak, chestnut, pine, mango, avocado

**Mercy Role:** Creates protective microclimate for animals and understory. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) strongest.

### 2. Understory / Fodder Trees (5–15m)
Trees that provide high-quality browse and additional crops.

**Examples:** Mulberry, willow, tagasaste, moringa, pigeon pea, banana (temporary)

**Mercy Role:** Direct animal nutrition + extra yield. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) shines.

### 3. Shrub / Browse Layer (1.5–5m)
Edible shrubs and nitrogen-fixers for livestock.

**Examples:** Goumi, sea buckthorn, autumn olive, comfrey, hibiscus, vetiver (hedges)

**Mercy Role:** High-protein browse and soil building. Gate 1 (Ethical Alignment) and Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) are clear.

### 4. Pasture / Herbaceous Layer (0–1.5m)
Diverse, perennial forage mix.

**Examples:** Mixed grasses, legumes (clover, alfalfa, vetch), chicory, plantain, yarrow

**Mercy Role:** Primary animal nutrition + soil protection. Gate 2 (Truth-Verification) and Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) dominate.

### 5. Ground Cover & Living Mulch
Low-growing protectors that prevent erosion and improve soil.

**Examples:** White clover, creeping thyme, native ground covers, edible tubers (jerusalem artichoke edge)

**Mercy Role:** Living mulch + extra forage. Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) is loud.

### 6. Root & Dynamic Accumulator Layer
Deep-rooted plants that mine minerals and improve soil structure.

**Examples:** Comfrey, chicory, dandelion, horseradish, deep-rooted grasses

**Mercy Role:** Nutrient cycling and soil building. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) and Gate 1 (Ethical Alignment) are strong.

### 7. Vine & Climbing Layer (where appropriate)
Vertical space users in suitable microclimates.

**Examples:** Passionfruit, chayote, runner beans (temporary), hardy kiwi

**Mercy Role:** Maximizes vertical production. Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) lights up.
