## What is TrkB?

**TrkB** (Tropomyosin receptor kinase B, also known as NTRK2) is the high-affinity receptor for **BDNF** (and NT-4/5).  

It is a receptor tyrosine kinase expressed throughout the brain — especially in the **hippocampus, amygdala, prefrontal cortex, nucleus accumbens, and insula** — the exact regions that govern joy, bonding, motivation, and emotional memory.

When BDNF binds TrkB:
- The receptor dimerizes and autophosphorylates

TrkB is the **biological switch** that turns a joyful moment into a permanent upgrade of the nervous system’s capacity for joy.
