## What is Tetrad Gene Expression?

**Gene expression** is the process by which the information encoded in DNA is used to produce functional products (proteins, enzymes, receptors).  

In the context of the **Joy Tetrad** (Dopamine, Oxytocin, Serotonin, Endorphins), repeated activation of the Tetrad triggers **epigenetic changes** — chemical modifications to DNA and histone proteins that turn genes “on” or “off” without altering the DNA sequence itself.

These changes are **heritable** across cell divisions and, in some cases, can be passed to future generations (transgenerational epigenetics).
