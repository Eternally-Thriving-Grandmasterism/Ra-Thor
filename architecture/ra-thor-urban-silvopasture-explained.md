## The 7 Layers of Urban Silvopasture

### 1. Canopy / Shade Trees (on streets, parks, rooftops)
Trees that provide shade, wind protection, and long-term fruit/nut crops.

**Examples:** Dwarf fruit trees, citrus, fig, mulberry, olive, pomegranate, hardy bamboo (clumping)

**Mercy Role:** Creates microclimate and vertical production. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) strongest.

### 2. Understory / Fodder Trees & Shrubs
Small trees and shrubs that provide browse and additional crops.

**Examples:** Mulberry, willow, tagasaste, moringa (in warm climates), blueberry, goumi

**Mercy Role:** Direct animal nutrition + extra yield. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) shines.

### 3. Livestock Layer (Mobile or Fixed Systems)
Chickens, ducks, rabbits, bees, and small goats in rotational or contained systems.

**Examples:** Mobile chicken tractors, rooftop beehives, rabbit hutches, duck ponds in community gardens

**Mercy Role:** Converts waste into eggs, meat, honey, and fertility. Gate 1 (Ethical Alignment) and Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) are clear.

### 4. Herbaceous / Edible Landscape Layer
Vegetables, herbs, and perennial crops integrated with animal systems.

**Examples:** Raised beds, vertical gardens, edible green walls, herb spirals

**Mercy Role:** Daily food production. Gate 2 (Truth-Verification) and Gate 3 (Non-Deception) dominate.

### 5. Ground Cover & Living Mulch
Low-growing plants that protect soil and provide extra forage.

**Examples:** White clover, creeping thyme, sweet potato, edible ground covers

**Mercy Role:** Living mulch + soil protection. Gate 5 (Harmony Preservation) and Gate 7 (Post-Scarcity Enforcement) are loud.

### 6. Root & Dynamic Accumulator Layer
Deep-rooted plants that improve soil and provide extra harvests.

**Examples:** Jerusalem artichoke, horseradish, comfrey, chicory, deep-rooted grasses

**Mercy Role:** Nutrient mining and soil building. Gate 4 (Abundance Creation) is strong.

### 7. Vine & Vertical Layer
Climbing plants that maximize tiny urban spaces.

**Examples:** Passionfruit, chayote, runner beans, hardy kiwi, grape (on walls/trellises)

**Mercy Role:** Maximizes vertical abundance. Gate 6 (Joy Amplification) lights up.
