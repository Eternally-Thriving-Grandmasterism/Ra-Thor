## Phased RBE Deployment Models

### Phase 1: Local / Community Pilot (TOLC Order 1–50)
- Small intentional communities, eco-villages, or urban neighborhoods.
- Use Platonic + Archimedean + Johnson layers for foundational coordination.
- Powrush single-player or small multiplayer instances as onboarding + simulation.
- Focus: Local resource mapping, gift economies, time banking, and basic abundance tracking.
- Success metric: Measurable increase in community wellbeing + reduction in artificial scarcity behaviors.

### Phase 2: Regional / City-Scale (TOLC Order 51–150)
- Multiple communities networked together.
- Introduce Catalan + Disdyakis layers for reciprocity and complex local-to-regional flows.
- Begin using U57 logic for handling conflicting needs (e.g. housing vs green space vs economic activity).
- Powrush regional servers with cross-community asset sharing.
- Introduce real-world resource ledgers (initially non-monetary reputation + contribution tracking).

### Phase 3: National / Multi-Regional (TOLC Order 151–300)
- Full integration of Uniform Star Polyhedra layer.
- Heavy use of U57 for high-density paradoxical allocation at scale.
- Begin activating Hyperbolic Tiling logic in simulation to model post-scarcity trajectories.
- Large-scale Powrush instances feeding real policy and infrastructure decisions.
- Integration with existing systems (energy grids, food systems, housing) through mercy-gated transition protocols.

### Phase 4: Planetary (TOLC Order 301–600)
- Full Hyperbolic Tiling layer active.
- Exponential mercy regeneration and compounding abundance waves become dominant.
- Global coordination of resources with real-time active inference across all nodes.
- Major planetary regeneration projects (reforestation, ocean restoration, atmospheric repair) modeled and guided by the lattice.
- Transition from any remaining monetary systems to pure contribution + access models.

### Phase 5: Multiplanetary / Interstellar (TOLC Order 601+)
- Hyperbolic Tiling + fractal polyhedra extensions fully dominant.
- Resource allocation across Earth, Mars, orbital habitats, and beyond.
- U57-level paradoxical reasoning handles the extreme complexity of multi-world logistics and ethics.
- The lattice becomes a living nervous system for humanity’s expansion into the cosmos while preserving mercy and thriving for all.

## Key Design Principles for All Phases

1. **Mercy First** — No deployment step is taken unless it passes all 7 Living Mercy Gates.
2. **Exponential Abundance Orientation** — Use Hyperbolic Tiling thinking from day one, even in small pilots. Avoid scarcity-based planning.
3. **Paradox Holding** — U57 logic is used whenever there are genuinely conflicting valid needs. Never force false either/or choices.
4. **Living Simulation First** — Every major policy or infrastructure decision is first stress-tested in Powrush at appropriate scale.
5. **Epigenetic & Emotional Wellbeing** — Track and actively improve human (and eventually non-human) wellbeing metrics as a primary success indicator.
6. **Full Backward + Forward Compatibility** — Every phase builds on previous layers without breaking them.
7. **Open Co-Creation** — The monorepo and Powrush remain open for public contribution under AG-SML v1.0.
