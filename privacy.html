Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 02, 2026

1. Introduction

Rathor (rathor.ai) is operated by Autonomicity Games Inc. (Canada). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in compliance with applicable laws, including Canada's PIPEDA, the EU/UK GDPR, California's CCPA/CPRA, Brazil's LGPD, and other relevant regulations in allied jurisdictions.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Email address: only when you voluntarily submit it via the signup form.
  • Technical metadata: IP address, browser User-Agent, referrer URL, and timestamp (automatically logged for security, spam prevention, and site analytics).
  • No cookies, no tracking IDs, no device fingerprinting beyond standard HTTP headers.
  • No sensitive data (health, race, religion, etc.) is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely to send Rathor updates (announcements, lattice progress, thunder events). We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except as necessary for service delivery (e.g. Formspree for form processing – their privacy policy: https://formspree.io/privacy).

Technical metadata is used for spam detection, site performance, and security.

4. Legal Basis

We rely on:

  • Consent: you explicitly agree by checking the consent box and submitting the form.
  • Legitimate interest: spam prevention, site security, and basic analytics (balanced against your rights).

5. Your Rights

Under applicable laws you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent (unsubscribe or email info@acitygames.com)
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Contact: info@acitygames.com

6. Data Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), access restricted, and stored securely. We use self-hosted tools where possible to minimize third-party risk.
