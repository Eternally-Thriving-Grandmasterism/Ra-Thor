# Ra-Thor Onça-Eterna Jaguar Guardian — Character Bible
## Overview
Ra-Thor Onça-Eterna (Eternal Jaguar), Verdant Nexus Sentinel of Brazil’s living spirit fused with cosmic tech. 8 ft tall hyper-realistic cybernetic jaguar warrior, guardian of ecosystems & planetary harmony. Exact visual fidelity to reference image: sleek robotic jaguar head with glowing yellow eyes & black rosettes, intricate golden mandala engravings, green/yellow/blue/black camouflage armor with Brazilian flag motifs (blue orb chest containing white stars & curved band, golden ring pulsing energy). Living vine in left hand symbolizes infinite life force. Dynamic heroic pose in snowy mountains at golden-hour sunset.

## Lore
Born from Amazonian jaguar spirit + Earth-core nexus technology. Protects biomes from imbalance, restores life where needed. Positive protector archetype — never destructive to nature.

## Core Systems

- Symbiosis: Armor camo adapts to any environment; vine is symbiotic bio-mechanical extension.
