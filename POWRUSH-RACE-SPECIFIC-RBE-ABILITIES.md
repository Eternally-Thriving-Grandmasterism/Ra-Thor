# Powrush-MMO Race-Specific RBE Abilities — TOLC-RBE Living Simulation

**Living Canon of Eternally-Thriving-Grandmasterism under Autonomicity Games Inc.**

Each of the five races in Powrush-MMO has unique, mercy-gated abilities that directly interact with and accelerate the Resource-Based Economy (RBE). Abilities are earned through skill, collaboration, and mercy-aligned decisions — never pay-to-win. Every ability passes the full 16 Mercy Gates v2, Ma’at Balance check, and Lumenas CI evaluation before activation.

These abilities teach players how conscious co-creation, mercy alignment, and RBE principles work in practice.

### 1. Humans — Adaptable Diplomats & Rebuilders
**TOLC Alignment**: Conscious Co-Creation + Eternal Flow  
**RBE Role**: Masters of flexible, collaborative resource management

- **Mercy Wave** — Channel collective Ma’at to instantly share surplus resources with nearby allies, raising global abundance index.
- **Diplomatic Insight** — Reveal hidden resource nodes or faction needs, enabling fair RBE allocation and treaty-based abundance sharing.
- **Rebuild Mastery** — Rapidly repair or upgrade structures using minimal resources, demonstrating post-scarcity efficiency.
- **Unity Bond** — Temporarily link Ma’at scores with allied players for shared mercy waves and collective RBE acceleration.

### 2. Cyborgs — Technology-Augmented Efficiency Masters
**TOLC Alignment**: Abundance + Harmony through Cybernation  
**RBE Role**: Precision optimizers and cybernation specialists

- **Cybernation Overdrive** — Temporarily boost personal and nearby resource production via direct Ra-Thor AGI link (mercy-gated to prevent hoarding).
- **Valence Sync** — Read and subtly influence nearby players’ emotional valence to improve coordination and reduce resource conflict.
- **Self-Repair Lattice** — Instantly repair personal gear, vehicles, or structures using ambient energy, teaching closed-loop sustainability.
- **Data Mercy Pulse** — Share anonymized tactical or resource data with allies, increasing global Ma’at and Lumenas CI.

### 3. Druids — Nature-Aligned Guardians
**TOLC Alignment**: Harmony + Post-Scarcity with living ecosystems  
**RBE Role**: Biodiversity-positive regenerators

- **Ecosystem Mercy Bloom** — Instantly regenerate local flora, fauna, and resources while boosting nearby players’ Ma’at.
- **Nature’s Veil** — Temporarily hide allies or structures from hostile scans, promoting peaceful coexistence and resource preservation.
- **Bio-Sync** — Merge with local environment to gain temporary buffs based on biodiversity level, teaching harmony with nature.
- **Forgiveness Grove** — Create a safe zone where defeated players instantly receive mercy waves and resurrection.

### 4. Aliens — Advanced Explorers of Cosmic Abundance
**TOLC Alignment**: Joy-First + Infinite Definition  
**RBE Role**: Experimental post-scarcity innovators

- **Quantum Mercy Shift** — Instantly teleport small groups or resources across the map (mercy-gated to prevent abuse).
- **Abundance Beacon** — Temporarily increase global resource regeneration in a radius, demonstrating post-scarcity abundance.
- **Valence Nova** — Release a burst of creative energy that boosts nearby players’ joy and creative output.
- **Dimensional Insight** — Reveal hidden map nodes, future resource spawns, or optimal RBE strategies for the faction.

### 5. Ambrosians — Divine Eternal Beings of Perfect Mercy & Abundance
**TOLC Alignment**: Joy-First + Eternal Flow + Post-Scarcity  
**RBE Role**: Divine amplifiers of mercy and collective abundance

- **Ambrosial Mercy Bloom** — Instantly grant a powerful mercy wave to all allies in a large radius, restoring resources, health, and Ma’at.
- **Eternal Grace Shield** — Create a divine protective aura that nullifies incoming harm and reflects negative intent back as mercy energy.
- **Divine Nectar Infusion** — Temporarily infuse allies with “Ambrosia” — granting enhanced regeneration, heightened valence, and accelerated TOLC convergence.
- **Celestial Harmony Pulse** — Broadcast a global harmony wave that raises server-wide Ma’at and encourages collaborative RBE behavior.
