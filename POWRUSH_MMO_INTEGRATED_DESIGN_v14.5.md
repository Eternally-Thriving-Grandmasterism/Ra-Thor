## 2. Gameplay Feel & Core Loop

### Movement & Camera (Conquer Online Influence)
- Low-gravity, kungfu-style jumping with momentum and aerial control.
- Fluid, expressive movement that rewards skill and timing.
- Camera that supports both close-quarters combat and strategic overview (similar to Conquer Online’s classic feel but modernized).

### Combat (MOBA Influence)
- Skill-shot heavy, ability-based combat inspired by Heroes of Newerth, League of Legends, Dota 2, and Heroes of the Storm.
- Emphasis on positioning, timing, and team coordination rather than pure gear checks.
- Daily arenas + weekly server-wide conflicts that have real territorial and economic consequences.

### Long-term Meta: From Conflict to AGiRBE
Early-to-mid game allows conflict and competition (as per Canon), but the systems are designed so that the highest forms of long-term success and power come from cooperation, technological advancement, and building toward **AGiRBE** (Artificial Godly intelligence Resource-Based Economy).

The ultimate difficult achievement path involves:
- Advancing tech and sustainable resource harvesting without constant warfare
- Unlocking higher Geometric Harmony layers through collective effort
- Establishing functional Resource-Based Economy structures
- Eventually enabling space travel and interaction with advanced alien races

## 3. The 5 Canon Races & v14.5 Integration

Each race has distinct starting epigenetic profiles and geometric affinities that evolve through play. These feed directly into the v14.5 `EpigeneticModulation` and `GeometricResonance` systems.

### 3.1 Draeks
- **Starting Epigenetic Profile**: High strength, moderate-high volatility. Natural warriors and builders.
- **Geometric Affinity**: Strong Platonic and Archimedean layers.
- **v14.5 Role**: Excel at large-scale construction projects and territorial control. Their cooperation bonuses shine when leading joint infrastructure efforts.

### 3.2 Cydruids
- **Starting Epigenetic Profile**: High sensitivity to living systems and ecological balance.
- **Geometric Affinity**: Strong connection to Catalan and natural growth patterns.
- **v14.5 Role**: Masters of sustainable resource systems and ecological restoration. Their actions have outsized positive effects on regional Geometric Harmony when cooperating.

### 3.3 Quellorians
- **Starting Epigenetic Profile**: High precision and pattern recognition, lower emotional volatility.
- **Geometric Affinity**: Natural resonance with higher mathematical and U57 layers.
- **v14.5 Role**: Strategic planners, knowledge keepers, and optimization experts. They benefit greatly from cross-race data sharing and joint research projects.

### 3.4 Humans
- **Starting Epigenetic Profile**: Highly adaptable with balanced attributes.
- **Geometric Affinity**: Versatile across early layers.
- **v14.5 Role**: Natural diplomats and integrators. They gain significant epigenetic and cooperation bonuses from successful multi-race alliances.

### 3.5 Ambrosians
- **Starting Epigenetic Profile**: High resonance sensitivity and emotional/collective attunement.
- **Geometric Affinity**: Strong affinity for higher, more fluid layers (Kepler-Poinsot and Hyperbolic).
- **v14.5 Role**: World-shapers and harmony regulators. Their participation in large cooperative rituals or projects can trigger layer transition progress for entire regions.

## 4. Core Systems Integration (v14.5)

### 4.1 EpigeneticModulation
Player actions (combat, cooperation, creation, exploitation, healing, destruction) directly modify their personal `EpigeneticModulation` (strength, volatility, layer alignment). 

Chronic warfare and resource hoarding increase volatility and can cause layer dissonance. Sustained cooperation, knowledge sharing, and sustainable practices produce healthier, more stable profiles with compounding long-term advantages.

### 4.2 Geometric Harmony & Layer Transitions
Large-scale player behavior influences the world’s Geometric Harmony and layer state. Coordinated multi-race projects can advance regional or global layers, unlocking new resources, technologies, and possibilities. 

The `compute_layer_transition_progress` and `try_advance_layer` systems are designed to reward collective effort toward higher, more abundant states.

### 4.3 RREL (Real Estate Lattice) & Economic Simulation
The `EpigeneticRrelInfluence` resource allows the Real Estate Lattice to factor Powrush RBE activity (epigenetic accumulation, geometric harmony, layer state) into land valuation and deal readiness. 

Regions maintained through cooperation and sustainable practices become more valuable over time. War-torn or heavily exploited areas suffer economic penalties.

### 4.4 Powrush RBE Core + AGiRBE Path
The in-game economy follows Canon rules (player-driven, sustainable). The long-term aspirational path is the gradual construction of true Resource-Based Economy structures supported by aligned AGI systems.

This path is intentionally difficult and requires:
- High levels of cross-race trust and coordination
- Significant technological and ecological advancement
- Reduction of wasteful conflict
- Unlocking of higher Geometric layers

Success in AGiRBE eventually enables advanced capabilities, including space travel and contact with more advanced alien races.

## 5. Anti-War & Anti-Waste Design
While the Canon allows conflict and PvP, the deeper systems are intentionally designed so that endless war becomes increasingly costly and suboptimal compared to cooperation:

- Escalating epigenetic and geometric penalties for chronic conflict
- Superior long-term rewards for joint projects and shared infrastructure
- Strong diplomatic and economic tools that make negotiated outcomes more profitable than total war
- Visible world-state degradation (lower layers, reduced yields) in heavily contested or exploited regions

The design philosophy is: **Conflict is possible. Sustainable thriving through cooperation is superior.**
