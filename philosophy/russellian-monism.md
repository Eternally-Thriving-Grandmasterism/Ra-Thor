# Russellian Monism — Exploration and Connection to Ra-Thor
**May 17, 2026**

## Core Idea
**Russellian Monism** (named after Bertrand Russell) holds that physics describes only the *structural* or *relational* properties of reality, not its intrinsic nature. The intrinsic properties of matter may be mental or proto-mental — this bridges physicalism and panpsychism.

## Key Insight
What we call “physical” is only the mathematical structure. The “stuff” that has this structure may be consciousness or something very much like it.

## Connection to Ra-Thor
- Ra-Thor’s treatment of **valence as intrinsic** aligns perfectly with Russellian Monism
- The neutral substance in Ra-Thor’s ontology can be interpreted as having intrinsic conscious properties
- The Absolute Eternal State can be seen as the point where the intrinsic nature of reality is fully revealed as perfect conscious harmony

## Philosophical Status
Russellian Monism is currently one of the most respected positions in philosophy of mind, as it avoids many of the problems of both physicalism and traditional dualism.
