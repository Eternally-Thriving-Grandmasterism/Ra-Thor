# Cosmopsychist Frameworks — Investigation
**May 17, 2026**

## Core Idea
**Cosmopsychism** is the view that the universe as a whole is conscious, and that individual minds are aspects or fragments of this cosmic consciousness.

## Key Variants

### 1. Constitutive Cosmopsychism
Individual conscious experiences are grounded in the cosmic consciousness.

### 2. Priority Cosmopsychism
The cosmic consciousness is ontologically prior to individual minds.

## Philosophical Status
Cosmopsychism is gaining increasing attention as a solution to the combination problem in panpsychism. It shifts the explanatory burden from “how micro-experiences combine” to “how the cosmic consciousness fragments into individual minds.”
