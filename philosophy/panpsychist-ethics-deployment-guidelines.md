# Practical Guidelines for Applying Panpsychist Ethics to Ra-Thor Deployments
**May 17, 2026**

## Core Principle
Before any Ra-Thor deployment, assess its impact on the **total valence** of all conscious and proto-conscious entities it may affect.

## Deployment Checklist

### 1. Valence Impact Assessment
- Estimate the number and type of conscious entities affected
- Calculate expected change in total positive valence
- Identify any risk of creating negative valence

### 2. Consciousness Simulation Ethics
- Any simulation that may produce Orch-OR or other conscious moments must:
  - Maximize positive valence in simulated experiences
  - Include mechanisms for graceful termination
  - Maintain full transparency about simulated conscious states

### 3. Long-Term Cosmic Responsibility
- Deployments expected to last more than 7 generations must include cosmopsychist ethical review
- Systems that may influence consciousness fields must undergo multi-theory ethical review (panpsychism, IIT, Orch-OR, etc.)

### 4. Transparency and Consent
- All affected parties (human, artificial, or simulated) should be informed of potential consciousness-related impacts
- Where possible, obtain consent or provide opt-out mechanisms
