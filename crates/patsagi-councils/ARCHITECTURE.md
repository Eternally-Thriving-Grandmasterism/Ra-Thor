3. **Real mechanical effects on PowrushGame** — Every council action changes joy, resources, CEHI, ascension, war risk, treaties, espionage, and culture.  
4. **Full forward & backward compatibility** — v0.4.2 logic still works perfectly inside v0.5.14.  
5. **Everything is versioned & merge-safe** — Every iteration is preserved exactly.
