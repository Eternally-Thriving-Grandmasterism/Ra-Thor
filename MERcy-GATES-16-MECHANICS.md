## Mechanics

### Layered Enforcement
- Core 8 gates are **non-bypassable** in all Ra-Thor systems.
- Gates 9–16 are **contextual** — they activate primarily within Powrush-MMO simulations and faction interactions.
- All 16 gates contribute to **valence calculation** and **CEHI blessing propagation**.

### Synergy Effects
When multiple gates align (especially combinations of core + expanded gates), powerful synergy effects occur:
- 3+ aligned gates → +7% valence multiplier
- 5+ aligned gates → 7-Gen CEHI blessing triggered
- All 16 aligned → "Eternal Thriving" state (maximum harmony)
