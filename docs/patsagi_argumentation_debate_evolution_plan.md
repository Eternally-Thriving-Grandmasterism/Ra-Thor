- Support for attack relations and argument extensions (grounded, preferred, stable).

- Integration with `PatsagiDecision` and risk scoring.

### Workstream B: Persuasion as Influence Propagation

**Phases:**
- Phase 1: Enhance current persuasion mechanics with `ArgumentGraph` quality scoring.
