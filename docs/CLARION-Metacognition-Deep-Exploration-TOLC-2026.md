This design gives CLARION exceptionally high psychological plausibility for human-like metacognition (e.g., knowing when you don’t know, switching strategies, extracting rules from experience).  

**Deep Mechanics of CLARION Metacognition**  
1. **Bottom-Up Monitoring**  
   - The implicit level continuously generates activations and associations.  
   - Metacognitive rules in the top level monitor bottom-level performance metrics (e.g., success rate, confidence, conflict).  
   - When the implicit level struggles (high uncertainty or low activation), the metacognitive layer triggers explicit intervention.  

2. **Top-Down Control**  
   - Explicit metacognitive rules decide when to:  
     - Extract new symbolic rules from successful implicit traces (rule extraction).  
     - Switch between implicit and explicit modes.  
     - Adjust parameters of the implicit level (e.g., learning rate, temperature).  
     - Initiate subgoals or strategy changes.  

3. **Rule Extraction Process**  
   - Implicit level solves a problem through neural reinforcement.  
   - Metacognitive layer detects success and extracts a symbolic rule that captures the implicit solution.  
   - This rule is added to the top level, enabling faster, more generalizable future performance.  

4. **Metacognitive Regulation Functions**  
   - **Self-monitoring**: Tracks performance of both levels (accuracy, speed, consistency).  
   - **Self-regulation**: Adjusts cognitive parameters dynamically.  
   - **Strategy selection**: Chooses between implicit intuition and explicit deliberation.
