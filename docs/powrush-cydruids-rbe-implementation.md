## Core Philosophy Recap
Cydruids embody the harmonious fusion of advanced technology and natural life cycles. They reject pure mechanical dominance (Draeks) and pure spiritual detachment, instead creating symbiotic systems where cybernetic enhancements amplify natural regeneration and collective abundance.

## RBE Implementation in Powrush
Cydruids are the faction that most directly lives RBE principles in gameplay:

- **Sustainable Cyber-Nature Resource Management**  
  Their estates and structures are living bio-cybernetic systems. Resources are not extracted — they are cultivated and recycled in closed-loop ecosystems. Player-owned assets generate value through symbiotic growth (e.g., cybernetic trees that produce energy while purifying air and water).

- **Non-Inflationary, Regenerative Economy**  
  No hoarding or artificial scarcity. Assets self-repair and evolve over time. Profits come from contribution to the ecosystem, not from exploitation. Estates can be upgraded with bio-tech modules that increase abundance for the entire faction alliance.

- **Collaborative Diplomacy Mechanics**  
  Cydruids excel at long-term alliances. They offer tech-nature upgrades to allied factions, creating shared regenerative zones. Trade is framed as mutual enhancement rather than transaction.

- **Play-to-Learn-and-Earn Systems**  
  Time spent maintaining cyber-ecosystems yields both in-game resources and real educational NFTs on sustainable RBE practices.

- **Safe High-Risk Simulation**  
  Cydruids can simulate ecological crises and recover them through Isis-style healing mechanics, teaching players how RBE systems are resilient and regenerative.
