### 2. Characteristic Scales

### 3. Non-Dimensionalization

- \(\mathbf{u}^* = \mathbf{u}/U\)

- \(T^* = (T - T_0)/\Delta T\)

Substitute into the momentum equation. The viscous term becomes:
\[
\frac{\nu}{U L} \nabla^{*2} \mathbf{u}^*

The dimensionless group that appears is the inverse of the Reynolds number.

\[
