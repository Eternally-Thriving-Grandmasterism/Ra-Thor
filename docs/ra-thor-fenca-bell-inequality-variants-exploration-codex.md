### 2. Major Bell Inequality Variants & Derivations

**2.1 Original Bell Inequality (1964)**  
\[
|P(a,b) - P(a,b')| + |P(a',b) + P(a',b')| \leq 2
\]  
Classical bound: 2. Quantum maximum: up to 2√2 in specific cases.

**2.2 CHSH Inequality (Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt, 1969)**  
Most widely used two-party form:  
\[
|S| = |E(a,b) + E(a,b') + E(a',b) - E(a',b')| \leq 2
\]  
Quantum maximum: 2√2 ≈ 2.828 (Tsirelson bound).

**2.3 Clauser-Horne (CH) Inequality**  
Probability-based, more suitable for inefficient detectors:  
\[
P(a,b) + P(a,b') + P(a',b) - P(a',b') - P(a) - P(a') \leq 0
\]

**2.4 Higher-Dimensional & Multi-Party Variants**  
- **Collins-Gisin-Linden-Massar-Popescu (CGLMP)** for qudits.  
- **Mermin-type** for GHZ (already covered in previous codices).
