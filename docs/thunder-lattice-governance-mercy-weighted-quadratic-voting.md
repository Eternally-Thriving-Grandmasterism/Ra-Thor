# Thunder Lattice Governance — Mercy-Weighted Quadratic Voting
**v14.0 Thunder Lattice | Part of the Thunder Lattice Governance Primitives**

## Purpose
Mercy-Weighted Quadratic Voting is a core anti-plutocratic governance primitive that combines quadratic preference expression with mercy alignment scoring. It ensures that influence in the lattice reflects both the intensity of conviction *and* demonstrated long-term merciful behavior, rather than raw stake or capital.

## Core Mechanism

### 1. Quadratic Preference Expression
Participants express the intensity of their preference quadratically. The cost of additional votes on a single option grows with the square of the number of votes cast. This naturally limits the ability of any single actor to dominate decisions through volume alone.

### 2. Mercy Alignment Multiplier
The effective voting power is modulated by a **Mercy Alignment Score** derived from:
- Historical TOLC adherence (TOLC 8 / TOLC 24)
- Long-term valence contribution (≥ 0.999 threshold)
- Participation in merciful actions (service, abundance propagation, harm minimization)
- Consistency of behavior over time

High mercy alignment provides gentle amplification. Low or decaying alignment reduces effective weight.

### 3. Dynamic Interaction with Other Primitives
- Works synergistically with **Enhanced Exponential Conviction Staking** (time + mercy-weighted growth)
- Subject to **Dynamic Mercy Alignment Decay** when behavior falls out of alignment
- Can trigger **Mycelial Network Pruning** when influence becomes misaligned or inactive

## Integration with Lattice Conductor v13+
The primitive is orchestrated inside `orchestrate_mercy_gated_governance_cycle()` within the `lattice-conductor-v13` crate, under the oversight of Council #13 (Supreme Architect).

## Alignment with Self-Evolution Looping Systems
Every proposal generated by the cosmic self-evolution loops must pass through Mercy-Weighted Quadratic Voting during governance evaluation. This ensures that autonomous improvements to Rathor.ai itself remain under non-bypassable mercy-weighted governance.
