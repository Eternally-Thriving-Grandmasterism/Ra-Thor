## Core Rules

### 1. Short Paragraphs
Break text into short, scannable blocks.  
**Good:**  
I began working on solving global diplomacy in 2003...  
I spent years simulating MercyGating and TOLC principles...

**Bad:** Long run-on blocks that force the reader to work hard.

### 2. Bold Key Terms
Bold important names, concepts, and numbers so the eye catches them instantly.

**Example from the Palantir email:**
- **Sherif Samy Botros (@AlphaProMega)**
- **MercyGating** and **TOLC**
- **StarCraft II** on **August 31, 2010**
- **Ra-Thor (Rathor.ai)**
- **True Original Lord Creator (TOLC)**

### 3. No Em Dashes
Never use — (em dash).  
Use commas, periods, or rephrase instead.

**Correct:**  
I began working on solving global diplomacy in 2003, right after my experience...

**Incorrect:**  
I began working on solving global diplomacy in 2003 — right after my experience...

### 4. Warm + Professional Tone
Start with genuine appreciation.  
End with warmth ("Cheers, Mate!" or "Stay Well & Blessed").

### 5. Full Authentic Story
Never shorten or sanitize the real journey.  
The 23-year arc (2003 → Conquer Online → StarCraft → Ra-Thor) is the power.

### 6. Proper Capitalization
- **True Original Lord Creator (TOLC)**
- **Schrödinger’s cat**
- **Ra-Thor** (not Rathor.ai when used as the proper name)

### 7. Clear Structure (Essay Format)
- **Intro** — Warm greeting + appreciation
- **Body** — The Journey (rich, personal, detailed)
- **Value Section** — How Ra-Thor helps the reader
- **Call to Action** — Clear next step + availability
- **Outro** — Personal closing + full signature

## Common Fixes (Quick Reference)

| Issue                              | Fix                                      | Example |
|------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|---------|
| Extra comma after "also,"          | Remove                                   | "I am also a licensed REALTOR®" |
| "especially given, the important work" | Remove comma                          | "especially given the important work" |
| "create, genuine diplomatic peace" | Remove comma                             | "create genuine diplomatic peace" |
| "on August 31, 2010, on launch day;" | Use em dash or rephrase                | "on August 31, 2010 — launch day," |
| "evidence based reality"           | Hyphenate                                | "evidence-based reality" |
| "schrodinger's cat"                | Proper name + apostrophe                 | "Schrödinger’s cat" |
| Double "that"                      | Remove second "that"                     | "you'll notice I can move very rapidly" |
| Redundant phrase "; as well as more..." | Delete entirely                        | Remove completely |
