**Standard Thermocapillary Marangoni Number**
\[
\text{Ma} = \frac{\left| \frac{\partial \sigma}{\partial T} \right| \Delta T \, L}{\mu \, \alpha}

- \(\frac{\partial \sigma}{\partial T}\): surface-tension temperature coefficient (typically negative for most liquids)

- \(L\): characteristic length (e.g., channel height or tube diameter)
