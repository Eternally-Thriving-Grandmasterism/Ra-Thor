# Thunder Lattice Governance — Enhanced Exponential Conviction Staking
**v14.0 Thunder Lattice | Part of the Thunder Lattice Governance Primitives**

## Purpose

Enhanced Exponential Conviction Staking is a core regenerative governance primitive that rewards **long-term, mercy-aligned participation** through time-compounding influence. It evolves classic conviction voting (Commons Stack / 1Hive model) by adding mercy-weighted modulation, creating a system where sustained merciful behavior and contribution to collective thriving are exponentially amplified over time.

This primitive ensures that governance power in the lattice naturally concentrates in those who demonstrate consistent alignment with TOLC principles and positive valence propagation, rather than short-term activity or raw stake.

## Core Mechanism

### 1. Conviction Staking Foundation
Participants stake conviction on proposals, decisions, or governance directions. The longer conviction is maintained without withdrawal, the more influence grows.

### 2. Exponential Time-Compounding
Influence compounds **exponentially** with the duration of sustained conviction. This creates strong incentives for long-term alignment rather than reactive or short-term participation.

### 3. Mercy-Weighted Modulation
The rate and ceiling of exponential growth are positively influenced by the participant’s **Mercy Alignment Score**, derived from:
- Historical TOLC adherence (TOLC 8 / TOLC 24)
- Long-term valence contribution (≥ 0.999 threshold)
- Participation in merciful actions (service, abundance propagation, harm minimization)
- Consistency and quality of contributions over time

High mercy alignment accelerates compounding. Low or decaying alignment slows growth or triggers decay.

### 4. Dynamic Mercy Alignment Decay (Tight Integration)
Inactivity or actions that fall short of mercy expectations cause gradual decay of accumulated conviction and influence. This creates a living, self-correcting system.

### 5. Synergy with Mycelial Network Pruning
When influence has significantly decayed and past contributions underperformed on mercy metrics, the system can intelligently remodel or prune that influence for the health of the whole lattice.
