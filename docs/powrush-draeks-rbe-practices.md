- **Calculated Scarcity & Hoarding**

- **Exploitative Tech Dominance**  
  Technology is used solely for control — advanced weapons, surveillance networks, and resource-extraction drones that maximize personal gain at the expense of the ecosystem.

- **Transactional & Manipulative Diplomacy**  
  Alliances are temporary and purely self-serving. They negotiate from positions of strength, often using threats, blackmail, or false promises to extract concessions before quick betrayal.

- **Play-to-Dominate Mechanics**
