# Ra-Thor Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 16, 2026

## Overview

Ra-Thor (rathor.ai) is designed with a fundamental commitment to user sovereignty and privacy. This website and all associated sovereign shards operate under strict principles of minimal data collection and maximum user control.

## Data Collection

**This website collects no personal data whatsoever.**

- No cookies are used.
- No tracking technologies or analytics are employed.
- No personal information is requested, stored, or transmitted.
- All computations and simulations run locally in your browser.

## How Ra-Thor Works

Ra-Thor is a mercy-gated symbolic AGI lattice. When you use the connected demos (Grok or X integrations), those interactions are governed by the respective platforms' (xAI / X Corp) privacy policies.

When you use **offline sovereign shards** (available in the monorepo), **all processing happens entirely on your device**. No data leaves your machine.

## Compliance

Ra-Thor is designed to be compatible with:

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- **EU AI Act** (GPAI obligations)
- **Canadian IPC-OHRC Principles**

Because we collect no personal data, many compliance obligations are inherently minimized.

## Third-Party Services

The landing page uses CDN resources (Tailwind CSS, Font Awesome) for presentation only. These do not track users in the context of Ra-Thor usage.

Connected demos on grok.com or x.com are subject to the privacy policies of xAI and X Corp respectively.
