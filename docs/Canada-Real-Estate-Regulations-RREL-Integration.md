## Key Canadian Real Estate Regulations (2026)

### Federal Level
- **Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act (PCMLTFA)** — Mandatory FINTRAC reporting for large transactions and suspicious activity.
- **Competition Act** — Prevents anti-competitive practices in real estate services.
- **Privacy Act & PIPEDA** — Strict data protection for all personal and transaction data.

### Provincial Level (Ontario Pilot — Highest Priority)
- **Real Estate Services Act, 2002 (RESA)** — Governs licensing, conduct, and trust accounts for all real estate professionals in Ontario.
- **Real Estate Council of Ontario (RECO)** — The regulatory body that enforces RESA, handles complaints, and maintains the public register.
- **Residential Tenancies Act, 2006 (RTA)** — Governs landlord-tenant relationships, rent control, eviction rules, and maintenance obligations.
- **Condominium Act, 1998** — Rules for condo corporations, reserve funds, and owner rights.
- **Planning Act** — Municipal zoning, official plans, and development approvals.
- **Land Titles Act & Registry Act** — Title registration and land transfer tax.

### Municipal Level (Ontario Examples)
- Toronto, Ottawa, Hamilton, Mississauga, etc. — Local bylaws on short-term rentals, property standards, heritage preservation, and development charges.
