## Design Principles

- **Mercy-Gated**: All transformations respect explicit mercy alignment
- **Coherence First**: Healing triggers only when needed
- **Extensible**: Clean path toward full CGA
- **Professional**: Strong typing, documentation, and error handling

## Technology Decision

**nalgebra + Quaternions** was chosen because:
- Mature and actively maintained
- Excellent `UnitQuaternion` support
- Good balance of power and simplicity
- Provides a migration path to future geometric algebra
