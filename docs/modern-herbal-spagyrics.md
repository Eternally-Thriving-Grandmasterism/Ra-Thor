## Modern Step-by-Step Process
1. **Fermentation (Mercury extraction)**  
   The fresh or dried herb is fermented to release the volatile spirit/alcohol fraction. Modern methods use controlled yeast or natural fermentation with precise temperature and pH monitoring.

2. **Distillation (Sulfur extraction)**  
   The fermented material is gently distilled to capture the essential oils and aromatic compounds (the Sulfur/soul). Modern vacuum distillation preserves delicate volatiles at lower temperatures.

3. **Calcination (Salt extraction)**  
   The remaining plant matter is dried and calcined (ashed) at high temperature to obtain the pure mineral salt. Modern techniques use controlled ovens or muffle furnaces for consistent purity.

4. **Purification**  
   Each fraction is further refined:
   - Essential oil (Sulfur) may be redistilled.
   - Alcohol (Mercury) is rectified.
   - Salt is leached and recrystallized multiple times.

5. **Recombination (Coagula)**  
   The purified Sulfur, Mercury, and Salt are reunited in precise ratios to create the final spagyric tincture or elixir. This step is often performed under astrological timing or with intention to infuse the medicine with higher energetic qualities.
