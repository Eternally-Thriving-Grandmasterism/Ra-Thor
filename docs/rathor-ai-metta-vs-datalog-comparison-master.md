## Overview
**Datalog** is a declarative, logic-programming language — essentially a restricted, safe subset of Prolog optimized for **deductive databases** and recursive queries. It is widely used in modern systems (Soufflé, VLog, LogicBlox, etc.) for static analysis, graph querying, and knowledge-base reasoning.

**MeTTa** is the typed, functional-logic, self-modifying language of OpenCog Hyperon, designed for dynamic, introspective AGI on a hypergraph (Atomspace).

