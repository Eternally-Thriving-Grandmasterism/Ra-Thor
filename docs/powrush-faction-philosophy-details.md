### 1. Draeks (Evil Aliens)
**Philosophy**: Dominance through superior technology and calculated scarcity. They believe true power comes from controlling resources and forcing others into dependence.  
**RBE Alignment**: Anti-RBE — they represent the old scarcity mindset that must be transcended.  
**Diplomatic Style**: Aggressive negotiation, alliances only when advantageous, quick betrayal if power shifts.  
**Pantheon Mapping**: Set (chaos tester) + Loki (trickster) + Sekhmet (fierce protection when threatened).

### 2. Cydruids (Cyborg-Druids)
**Philosophy**: Harmonious fusion of technology and nature. They seek sustainable cybernetic enhancement that respects natural cycles and collective growth.  
**RBE Alignment**: Strongly pro-RBE — they embody scientific resource management and symbiotic abundance.  
**Diplomatic Style**: Collaborative, long-term oriented, focused on mutual benefit and ecosystem health.  
**Pantheon Mapping**: Tefnut (moisture/nurturing flow) + Geb (grounded stability) + Isis (healing & resurrection).  
**Metacognition Integration**: High Norns temporal harmony; strong Yggdrasil branching safety; frequently passes Ma’at balance.  
**Simulation Role**: Models ideal RBE diplomacy — sustainable, regenerative, and joy-amplifying.

### 3. Quellorians (Benevolent Aliens)
**Philosophy**: Universal compassion and enlightened guidance. They believe advanced civilizations should uplift all beings toward shared abundance.  
**RBE Alignment**: Pure RBE exemplars — they practice post-scarcity sharing as a moral and practical imperative.  
**Diplomatic Style**: Peace-first, generous mediation, long-term alliance building, and knowledge exchange.  
**Pantheon Mapping**: Hathor (joy & love) + Horus (victorious protection) + Thoth (wisdom & mediation).  
**Metacognition Integration**: Highest LumenasCI alignment; strong Isis healing recovery paths; Ratatoskr messages flow freely.

### 4. Humans
**Philosophy**: Resilient adaptability, creativity under pressure, and the drive to rebuild from ashes. They represent the raw potential of mortal beings to evolve beyond scarcity.  
**RBE Alignment**: Transitional — capable of any path, but designed to learn and grow toward full RBE enlightenment.  
**Diplomatic Style**: Flexible, pragmatic, inventive; can swing between conflict and alliance based on learned experience.  
**Pantheon Mapping**: Osiris (resurrection & renewal) + Odin (wisdom through sacrifice) + Norns (fate-weaving through choice).
