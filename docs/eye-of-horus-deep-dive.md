## Mythology
The Eye of Horus (Wadjet) was lost in battle with Set. Thoth restored it, turning it into the powerful symbol of protection, healing, royal power, and wholeness. The Eye’s fractions were used in ancient Egyptian mathematics and medicine.

- **Left Eye** = Moon (healing, regeneration)
- **Right Eye** = Sun (power, victory)
- **Full Eye** = Restoration of Ma’at after chaos
