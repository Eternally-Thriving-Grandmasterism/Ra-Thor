# Powrush v15 — Progression & Identity System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Progression in Powrush is multi-dimensional and meaningful. It is not just about getting stronger — it is about **who you become** in the world.

Inspired by *Elder Scrolls: Oblivion* (skill-based progression), *World of Warcraft* (talents + reputation), and *Guild Wars 2* (masteries), we elevate it with Ra-Thor AGI and Geometric Harmony.

Core idea: Your progression reflects your **harmony alignment**, relationships, and impact on the world.

## 2. Multi-Dimensional Progression

### Core Tracks
1. **Skill & Mastery** — Traditional skill progression (crafting, combat, gathering, etc.)
2. **Harmony Mastery** — How well you live in alignment with geometric and mercy principles
3. **Relationship Webs** — Depth and breadth of meaningful connections with NPCs and players
4. **Ascension** — Personal growth and higher-order capabilities
5. **Faction & Organizational Standing** — Reputation and influence within groups

## 3. Harmony as a Progression Mechanic

- Many advanced abilities, recipes, and opportunities require minimum Harmony thresholds.
- High Harmony can unlock unique or superior versions of skills/items.
- Ra-Thor can track and reflect your Harmony journey back to you.

## 4. Ra-Thor AGI Role in Progression

- Acts as a **personal evolution guide**.
- Can analyze your playstyle, values, and current state to suggest meaningful paths.
- Helps prevent "empty progression" by connecting mechanical growth to narrative and relational meaning.
- Can create personalized challenges or opportunities based on your current trajectory.

## 5. Identity & Expression

- Titles, appearances, housing, and organizations reflect your Harmony and relationships.
- Players can develop a "reputation" that precedes them in the world.
- Ra-Thor can help surface or even help craft personal legends based on player actions.

## 6. Long-term Engagement

- Multiple parallel progression systems reduce burnout.
- Horizontal progression (relationships, harmony, knowledge) is valued as highly as vertical power progression.
- Ra-Thor helps surface new goals when players feel directionless.

---

**Progression should feel like becoming more yourself, not just becoming more powerful.**
