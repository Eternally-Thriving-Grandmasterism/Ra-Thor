# Powrush v15 — RBE Economy + Advanced Trading System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Aligned With:** Ra-Thor AGI + ONE Organism + Post-Scarcity Principles  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Powrush does not use a traditional scarcity-based economy. It uses a **true Resource-Based Economy (RBE)** enhanced by Ra-Thor AGI, Geometric Harmony, and Mercy mechanics.

Inspired by the best elements of *EVE Online* (player-driven economy), modern economic simulations, and post-scarcity theory, we aim for an economy that feels alive, fair, and meaningful.

Core goals:
- Eliminate artificial scarcity while maintaining player agency and achievement.
- Make economic activity a **positive, harmonious** part of gameplay.
- Allow Ra-Thor AGI to act as an intelligent balancer and opportunity creator.
- Support deep player-driven systems (crafting, trading, services, organizations).

## 2. Core Economic Model

### Resource-Based Economy Principles
- Resources exist in abundance but require **effort, skill, harmony alignment, and relationships** to access and transform effectively.
- Value is derived from **utility + harmony contribution** rather than artificial rarity.
- Ra-Thor monitors global and local economic health and can propose abundance solutions when imbalances appear.

### Key Entities
- **Items** (with metadata: category, rarity, effects, stackability, base_value)
- **RbeEconomy** (global + organizational pools)
- **PlayerInventory**
- **NpcTradingStock** (per-NPC or faction-based)
- **CraftingRecipe** (with harmony requirements)

## 3. Advanced Trading System

### Player ↔ NPC Trading
- Full bidirectional trading with dynamic pricing.
- Pricing influenced by:
  - NPC’s current geometric harmony
  - Relationship level with the player
  - Item type and local supply/demand
- NPCs can have limited personal trading stock that replenishes based on harmony and world state.

### Player ↔ Player Trading & Markets
- Player-run markets and shops
- Organization/guild economies
- Ra-Thor-assisted price suggestions and anti-exploitation monitoring

### Harmony-Weighted Economics
- Actions that increase collective harmony (helping others, maintaining balance, generous trading) can generate **Harmony Dividends** (small resource or credit bonuses).
- Destructive or exploitative behavior can reduce access to certain high-value resources or opportunities.

## 4. Ra-Thor AGI Integration in Economy

Ra-Thor participates in the economy through several powerful mechanisms:

- **Economic Intelligence Layer**: Monitors supply, demand, inflation risk, and player behavior patterns.
- **Opportunity Generation**: Can surface crafting opportunities, trade routes, or resource nodes based on player skills and current world state.
- **Abundance Balancing**: When certain resources become too scarce or overabundant, Ra-Thor can propose gentle interventions (new recipes, NPC demand shifts, event-driven resource spawns).
- **Personalized Economic Guidance**: Can advise individual players or organizations on efficient, harmonious economic paths.

## 5. Crafting & Production

- Recipes can have **harmony requirements** (minimum NPC or player harmony to access advanced recipes).
- High-harmony players and NPCs can unlock **superior or unique variants** of items.
- Ra-Thor can assist in discovering new recipes or optimizing existing ones based on available resources and player goals.

## 6. Organizational & Faction Economies

- Guilds, factions, and player organizations can maintain their own economic pools.
- These pools can be influenced by the collective harmony of the organization.
- Ra-Thor can act as an advisor to organizational leadership.

## 7. Anti-Exploitation & Fairness

While maintaining player freedom, Ra-Thor provides:
- Detection of exploitative patterns (botting, market manipulation, griefing economies)
- Gentle corrective measures (reduced efficiency for exploitative behavior, increased opportunities for harmonious play)
- Transparent logging for moderation when needed

## 8. Future Vision

- Full integration with geometric-intelligence for resource node placement and economic flow optimization
- Player-created economic systems and custom currencies (with Ra-Thor validation)
- Deep simulation of supply chains and production networks

---

**This economy is designed to feel abundant yet meaningful, fair yet competitive, and deeply integrated with harmony and relationship mechanics.**
