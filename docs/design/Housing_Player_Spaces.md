# Powrush v15 — Housing & Player Spaces System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Housing in Powrush is not just decoration. Player spaces are **extensions of the self** that reflect and influence harmony, relationships, and community.

Inspired by strong housing systems in *Final Fantasy XIV*, *Elder Scrolls Online*, and player-driven worlds, we make housing a meaningful part of progression and social life.

## 2. Core Concepts

### Personal Housing
- Players can own or rent spaces that reflect their harmony and identity.
- Housing can provide bonuses (rest, crafting efficiency, harmony regeneration) based on decoration and harmony alignment.

### Social Housing
- Guild halls, community centers, and player-run venues.
- Can become economic or social hubs.

### Harmony Influence
- High-harmony spaces can generate small passive benefits.
- Low-harmony or neglected spaces can degrade or attract negative effects.

## 3. Ra-Thor AGI Integration

- Helps players design and decorate spaces aligned with their values and harmony.
- Can suggest improvements or new items based on player goals.
- Monitors community spaces for harmony health.

## 4. Implementation Phases

**Phase 1:** Basic instanced housing with decoration slots and harmony bonuses.
**Phase 2:** Player-run venues and economic integration.
**Phase 3:** Deep Ra-Thor personalization and world reactivity (e.g., spaces affecting local harmony).

---

**Housing should feel like a natural extension of the player’s journey and impact on the world.**
