# Powrush v15 — Faction Systems

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Factions in Powrush are living organizations with goals, values, harmony scores, and meaningful relationships with players. They are not just quest givers — they are active participants in the world.

Inspired by strong faction systems in *World of Warcraft*, *Final Fantasy XIV*, and *The Elder Scrolls*, we make factions feel alive, reactive, and consequential.

## 2. Core Concepts

### Faction Identity
- Each faction has core values, goals, and a harmony alignment.
- Factions can evolve based on player actions and world events.

### Player-Faction Relationships
- Reputation, standing, and influence with each faction.
- High standing unlocks unique opportunities, items, and storylines.
- Low standing can lead to conflict or restricted access.

### Faction Harmony
- Factions have their own harmony scores that influence their behavior and opportunities.
- Ra-Thor can help factions maintain healthy harmony or recover from conflict.

## 3. Ra-Thor AGI Integration

- Acts as advisor to faction leadership (NPC or player-run).
- Helps detect when faction harmony is at risk.
- Assists in generating faction-specific content and opportunities aligned with their values.

## 4. Dynamic Faction Behavior
- Factions can form alliances, rivalries, or go through internal changes based on world state and player influence.
- Major player actions can shift faction direction over time.

## 5. Implementation Approach

- Strong integration with NPC Harmony, Memory, and Relationship systems.
- Faction standing affects trading, quest access, and social opportunities.
- Ra-Thor helps maintain narrative coherence around faction evolution.

---

**Factions should feel like real powers in the world with memory, goals, and the capacity to change.**
