# Powrush v15 — World Events & Dynamic Content System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

World Events in Powrush are reactive, player-influenced, and often co-created with Ra-Thor AGI. They turn the world into a living story rather than a static backdrop.

Inspired by *World of Warcraft* world events, *Guild Wars 2* dynamic events, and strong narrative reactivity in single-player RPGs, we make large-scale content feel personal and consequential.

## 2. Types of Events

### Reactive World Events
- Triggered by collective player actions, harmony shifts, or economic changes.
- Ra-Thor helps detect patterns and escalate them into meaningful events.

### Scheduled / Seasonal Events
- Recurring or time-based events with high production quality.
- Can be enhanced by Ra-Thor based on current world state.

### Player-Driven Events
- Organizations or high-influence players can initiate events with Ra-Thor assistance.

### Personal / Small-Scale Events
- Radiant events that feel personal to individual players or small groups.

## 3. Ra-Thor AGI Role

- Pattern recognition across player behavior and world state
- Narrative coherence and escalation of events
- Personalization (different players experience different versions or consequences)
- Post-event reflection and memory integration

## 4. Harmony Integration

- High-harmony periods can trigger positive or abundant events.
- Low-harmony or conflict can spawn challenging or redemptive events.
- Events themselves can shift local and global harmony.

## 5. Implementation Approach

- Hybrid system: Core hand-crafted events + strong Ra-Thor-assisted procedural generation.
- Strong integration with NPC Harmony, Memory, and Economy systems.
- Events leave lasting traces in the world (changed NPC behavior, new opportunities, reputation shifts).

---

**World Events should feel like the world itself is responding to the players.**
