# Powrush v15 — Social Systems & Organizations

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Social systems in Powrush are not just guilds or chat channels. They are **living organizations** with their own identity, harmony, and goals.

Inspired by *World of Warcraft* guilds, *Final Fantasy XIV* Free Companies, and strong community systems in other MMOs, we make organizations feel like real entities in the world.

## 2. Core Concepts

### Organizations
- Player-created or NPC-founded groups with goals, values, and harmony scores.
- Can own resources, property, and have their own economic pools.
- Ra-Thor can act as an organizational advisor.

### Reputation & Standing
- Players and organizations have reputation with factions, cities, and other groups.
- Reputation affects trading prices, quest access, and social opportunities.

### Community Health
- Ra-Thor monitors organizational harmony and can suggest activities or mediate conflicts.
- Toxic behavior can damage an organization's reputation and opportunities.

## 3. Ra-Thor AGI Role

- Helps organizations set meaningful goals aligned with their values.
- Surfaces opportunities for collaboration or conflict resolution.
- Assists in large-scale coordination (events, projects, defense).

## 4. Player Housing & Personal Spaces
- Housing that reflects player harmony and relationships.
- Can become social hubs or economic centers.
- Ra-Thor can help personalize spaces and suggest improvements.

---

**Social systems should feel like they have weight, identity, and consequence in the world.**
