# Powrush v15 — Quests & Narrative System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Design Specification  
**Date:** June 2026

## 1. Vision

Quests and narrative in Powrush are reactive, personal, and co-created with Ra-Thor AGI.

Inspired by *The Witcher 3* (meaningful choices), *Final Fantasy XIV* (strong ongoing narrative), and *Elder Scrolls* (radiant quests), we make storytelling a living, player-shaped experience.

Key principles:
- Every player has a **unique story** that emerges from their actions, relationships, and harmony alignment.
- Ra-Thor acts as a **narrative intelligence** that helps maintain coherence while enabling emergence.
- Quests feel personal and consequential.

## 2. Quest Types

### Traditional Quests
- Hand-crafted story arcs with high production quality.

### Radiant & Procedural Quests
- Generated based on NPC needs, player history, current world state, and harmony levels.
- Ra-Thor helps ensure they feel meaningful rather than generic.

### Personal & Relationship Quests
- Quests that emerge from deep relationships with specific NPCs.
- Can span long periods and evolve based on player choices.

### World Event Quests
- Large-scale reactive events driven by collective player behavior and world harmony.

## 3. Ra-Thor AGI in Narrative

- **Memory & Context**: Maintains understanding of player history across quests.
- **Coherence Engine**: Helps ensure story threads connect meaningfully.
- **Emergence Support**: Detects interesting patterns in player behavior and turns them into narrative opportunities.
- **Personalization**: Adjusts tone, difficulty, and themes based on player values and harmony.

## 4. Choice & Consequence

- Major choices have lasting impact on NPCs, factions, and the world state.
- Ra-Thor tracks consequences across the world and can surface them later.
- Players can see (or feel) the ripple effects of their actions.

## 5. Implementation Approach

- Hybrid system: High-quality hand-crafted content + strong procedural generation assisted by Ra-Thor.
- Quests are data + state driven, with Ra-Thor filling gaps intelligently.
- Strong integration with the NPC Harmony and Relationship systems.

---

**Narrative should feel like it belongs to the player, while still being part of a living, coherent world.**
