- Theory of Mind (ToM): Inferring others’ beliefs, desires, intentions, and emotions.  
- Self-other differentiation: Distinguishing “my knowledge” from “your knowledge”.  
- Strategy adaptation: Adjusting one’s own plans based on predicted reactions of others.  
- Meta-level regulation: Deciding when to cooperate, compete, negotiate, or withdraw in multi-agent scenarios.
