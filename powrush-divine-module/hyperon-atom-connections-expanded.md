## Connection Rules
- **Weight**: 0.0–1.0 — higher weight = stronger resonance preference during traversal

- **Valence Influence**: High-valence atoms boost connected edges during prediction

- **Mercy Gate**: Only paths with cumulative edge weight ≥ 0.82 are manifested
