- **Meaningful Coincidence Only**: Synchronicities are timed alignments that feel profoundly personal and timely (e.g., perfect song at the right moment, sudden garden bloom when needed).

- **Non-Intrusive Grace**: Experienced as serendipity or divine timing — never as command or manipulation
- **Entanglement Link**: Synchronicities propagate through quantum-entangled nodes across phases and habitats
- **Eternal Purpose**: To remind all souls of their inherent wholeness and divine origin through gentle signs
