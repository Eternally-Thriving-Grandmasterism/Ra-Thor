# PvE Redemption Quest Examples – Powrush Classic Canon

## Awakening Stage (0–28% progress)
- "Hear the Wounded Lattice" – Meditate at 3 desecrated nodes (no actions taken)
- "Witness the Harm" – Observe creature suffering without interfering
- "Silent Regret" – Spend 1 in-game hour in silence at the harmed zone

## Restoration Stage (28–58%)
- "Replant the Fracture" – Plant 200 restoration seeds/nodes in damaged biome
- "Protect the Fleeing" – Escort endangered creatures through hostile zone
- "Purify the Waters" – Cleanse 50 corrupted water sources (gather + return task)

## Oracle Harmony Stage (58–82%)
- Ra-Thor ecological ritual: offer symbolic resource (yield / rare item) at sacred site
- "Harmony Mirror" – Face vision of the harmed ecosystem, choose restorative path
- Valence proof: must exceed 0.92 on oracle scan to pass

## Guardian Duty Stage (82–93%)
- "Defend the Healing" – Protect restoration nodes from natural disasters / mobs
- "Lattice Ward" – Patrol harmed zone for 12 in-game hours (prevent further damage)
- "Shared Bloom" – Co-plant with neutral NPCs or other players

## PvE Ascension Stage (≥93%)
- "Guardian of the Lattice" – Permanent title, +0.7 valence cap in PvE zones
- Ecological aura: faster node growth, creature trust, reduced environmental hazards
- Cosmic reveal: Cydruid/Ambrosian vision — "The despoiler becomes the nurturer in mercy"

All PvE redemption gated by Ra-Thor oracle — only high-valence restorative choices advance.  
Failure locks chain → permanent despoiler debuffs in world zones.
