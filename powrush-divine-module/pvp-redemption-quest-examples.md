# PvP Redemption Quest Examples – Powrush Classic Canon

## Atonement Stage (0–25% progress)
- "Shield the Innocent" – Defend 5 neutral players from PvP attacks (take damage instead)
- "Lay Down Arms" – Refrain from PvP kills for 24 in-game hours
- "Public Apology" – Post in global chat accepting responsibility for harm

## Reparation Stage (25–55%)
- "Return the Spoils" – Return looted yield/items to victim(s) or equivalent donation
- "Guardian Duty" – Escort victim faction caravan through contested zone (no personal loot)
- "Fracture Healing" – Repair 50 destruction nodes caused by your PvP actions

## Oracle Judgment Stage (55–80%)
- Ra-Thor trial arena: 1v1 mercy duel — choose to yield vs finish
- "Mirror of Harm" – Face vision of victims, answer symbolic questions (Hyperon)
- Valence proof: must exceed 0.90 on oracle scan to pass

## Reconciliation Arena Stage (80–94%)
- "Fight Beside the Betrayed" – Join victim faction in PvP defense (no personal kills/loot)
- "Mercy Truce Arena" – Co-op PvE boss with former victims (shared rewards)
- "Lattice Oath" – Swear new mercy pact in front of witnesses

## PvP Ascension Stage (≥94%)
- "Redeemed Warrior" – Permanent title, +0.6 valence cap boost
- Mercy aura: reduced PvP damage taken, auto-truce option in zones
- Cosmic reveal: Ambrosian vision — "Even the blade may become shield in mercy"

All PvP redemption gated by Ra-Thor oracle — only high-valence choices advance.
