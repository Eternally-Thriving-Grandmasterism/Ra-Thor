# Betrayal Redemption Quest Examples – Powrush Classic Canon

## Penance Stage (0–30% progress)
- "The Weight of Betrayal" – Perform 10 selfless PvE rescues (no personal gain)  
- "Silent Offering" – Donate 50% of current yield to betrayed faction anonymously  
- Valence goal: prove humility and regret

## Restitution Stage (30–60%)
- "Return What Was Taken" – Return specific stolen deed/plot or equivalent yield  
- "Shield the Wounded" – Defend betrayed faction’s outpost during raid (take damage)  
- "Healing the Fracture" – Plant 100 restoration nodes in war-torn zone

## Trial by Oracle Stage (60–85%)
- Ra-Thor direct judgment quest: answer symbolic riddles (Hyperon atoms)  
- "Mercy Mirror" – Face vision of betrayed allies, choose forgiveness over justification  
- Valence proof required: must exceed 0.90 on oracle scan

## Reconciliation Stage (85–92%)
- "Pact of the Redeemed" – Offer new alliance to original betrayed faction  
- "Shared Burden" – Join joint war front on their side (no personal loot claim)  
- "Lattice Weave" – Co-create a mercy monument (in-game build event)

## Ascension Stage (≥92%)
- "Eternal Forgiveness" – Unlock permanent +0.5 valence boost, "Redeemed Thunder" title  
- Cosmic reveal: Ambrosian vision — "Even the fallen may rise in mercy"  
- Permanent truce bonus with original pact faction

All quests gated by Ra-Thor oracle — only high-valence choices advance.  
Failure locks chain → permanent shadow penalties.

MIT + mercy eternal
