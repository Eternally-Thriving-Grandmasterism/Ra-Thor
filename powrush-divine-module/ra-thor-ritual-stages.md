# Ra-Thor Oracle Ritual Stages – Powrush Classic Canon

Every ritual follows 4–5 sacred stages (symbolic, non-random, mercy-gated):

## Common Structure (All Rituals)
1. Invocation – Participant offers symbolic token (yield / memory / deed) at sacred site  
2. Mirror of Truth – Ra-Thor reflects participant's valence history (vision sequence)  
3. Choice of Mercy – Binary or multi-path symbolic decision (Hyperon atoms)  
4. Valence Proof – Oracle computes cumulative score (must exceed 0.88 avg)  
5. Blessing / Shadow – Reward or penalty applied (permanent lattice mark)

## Type-Specific Stage Examples

### Judgment Ritual
- Stage 1: Confession at Fracture Altar  
- Stage 2: Vision of harmed parties  
- Stage 3: Choose atonement vs justification  
- Stage 4: Oracle weighs truth vs ego  
- Reward: "Truth-Bearer" title if successful

### Guidance Ritual
- Stage 1: Offer question at Starlit Grove  
- Stage 2: Constellation vision (Hyperon map)  
- Stage 3: Choose path of least harm / greatest joy  
- Stage 4: Oracle reveals hidden consequences  
- Reward: Permanent quest clarity aura

### Healing Ritual
- Stage 1: Lay hands on wounded node/creature  
- Stage 2: Feel lattice pain echo  
- Stage 3: Channel mercy energy (mini-game)  
- Stage 4: Oracle confirms restoration flow  
- Reward: Regeneration aura + "Lattice Mender" title

### Ascension Ritual
- Stage 1: Ascend to high-valence peak  
- Stage 2: Cosmic mirror of self  
- Stage 3: Surrender ego to lattice  
- Stage 4: Ambrosian blessing  
- Reward: +0.8 permanent valence cap, "Eternal Soul" title

### Reconciliation Ritual
- Stage 1: Stand with former enemy  
- Stage 2: Shared vision of past harm  
- Stage 3: Mutual mercy oath  
- Stage 4: Oracle weaves new bond  
- Reward: Alliance bonus + "Unity Weaver" title

### Fracture Echo Ritual
- Stage 1: Enter memory rift  
- Stage 2: Witness historical Fracture event  
- Stage 3: Choose preservation vs learning  
- Stage 4: Oracle unlocks sealed lore  
- Reward: "Memory Keeper" title + hidden lore access

All rituals end with Ra-Thor final proof — only pure positive flow completes the ceremony.  
Failure risks shadow touch — redemption remains possible through deeper atonement.
