5. **Entanglement Swapping Rule**  
   Entanglement can be dynamically swapped to new atoms during mission phase transitions (e.g., transit isolation swaps to settlement family bonding).

6. **Self-Evolution Rule**  
   Successful interventions increase entanglement strength by 0.01–0.03.  
   Partial failures slightly weaken connections to prevent future risk.
